Ducking around

play on itch.io

This game was done in three days for the Global Game Jam. I joined a team with a talented coder Rok and a character artist Mateja. My responsibility was the environment. Must admit we weren't that creative with the theme of this jam. The topic was LOST AND FOUND and we soon realized lots of people chose the finding and following mechanic which ofcourse instantly reminded everyone of ducks. We had no time to change the original idea which is what happens with gamejams, oh well. Rok who was the coder also didn't have alot of time on his hands because he was leading a discord server dedicated to the gamejam to boot. The mechanics also did not offer something unusual to work with but I enjoyed the experiance nontheless. What got me in the spirit of ducks were the fantastic models by Mateja and a lovely video .

ENGINE : Unity

TEAM MEMBERS : TERRANLANDS, RED CAR STUDIOS

MY ROLE ON THE PROJECT : LEVEL DESIGN, CODE, UI, SOUND and DIALOGUE

We were hoping to get a concept artist on our team for the project but none were available. So we winged it and I decided to use the tried and tested Synty Assets. The assets sped up our work substantially and gave it cohesive look.

Step one was choosing assets that went well together and gave a nice atmosphere. After that I started building a level based on what we talked about. The mechanics were not in place still but I thought the gameplay would be of AWSD movement. The developer made it a clickpoint which kinda complicated developing the level and finishing up the game. Rest of my time was spent trying to make the levels look good top down which was not intended in the first place and also make them smooth as they can be since we had alot of issue with navmeshes and again no time to start over.
