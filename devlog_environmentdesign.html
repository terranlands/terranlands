Ducking around

play on itch.io

This game was done in three days for the Global Game Jam. I joined a team with Rok and a character artist Mateja. My responsibility was the environment. We were hoping to get a concept artist on our team for the project but none were available. So we winged it and I decided to use the tried and tested Synty Assets. The assets sped up our work substantially and gave it cohesive look. I built up three different sectors and scattered the ducklings around the map. You can play the game on itchio.

ENGINE : Unity

TEAM MEMBERS : MELITA, ROK, MATEJA

MY ROLE ON THE PROJECT : ENVIRONMENT

Step one was choosing assets that went well together and gave a nice atmosphere. After that I started building a level based on what we talked about. The mechanics were not in place still but I thought the gameplay would be of AWSD movement. The developer made it a clickpoint unexpectedly which then in turn had to be reflected in the level. Rest of my time was spent trying to make the levels look good both top down and third person. If I was doing an environment like this again I would give characters wider paths to walk on.
